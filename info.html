Ceibo

Our Head Chef

We are pleased to have Pablo Arduino as our Head Chef. Hailing from Uruguay, he has accomplished alot in the culinary world. Graduating from IGA at the top of his class at the young age of 18, he has since traveled from Argetina to France and so many places inbetween. His travels has given him a sense of diversity and a flavor profile unmatched. Upon moving to Minnesota he has decided it's time to settle down and calls Ceibo his home.
